What makes art ART

Many people do not consider themselves artists, often feeling that art requires a special talent or training that they lack. However, I believe that everyone possesses the innate ability to create art and should be encouraged to do so.
Art is not confined to traditional forms like painting or sculpture; it can be expressed through various mediums such as writing, cooking, gardening, or even problem-solving. The act of creating art is a powerful means of self-expression and personal growth,
allowing individuals to convey emotions, tell stories, and connect with others on a deeper level. By embracing our creative potential, we can discover new passions, reduce stress, and enhance our overall well-being.
Ultimately, the world would be a richer, more vibrant place if everyone recognized and nurtured their inner artist.
